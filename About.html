About ANITS

  • ANITS (Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Sciences) is a renowned engineering college situated in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India.
  • Established in 2001, it offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines.
  • With modern facilities and experienced faculty, ANITS emphasizes holistic development and fosters a culture of innovation and research among its students.
  • Committed to excellence, ANITS prepares students to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society.
  • ANITS provides industry exposure through internships, workshops, and guest lectures to prepare students for real-world challenges.
  • The college encourages extracurricular activities and community engagement to nurture well-rounded individuals.
  • ANITS has collaborations with leading industries and research organizations to facilitate practical learning and innovation.
